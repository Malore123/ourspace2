Web Media Production BA(Hons)

At Web Media you will learn how to create professional web content and services. You will take part in both practical workshops and theoretical lessons, taught by experienced industry leaders, and guests from companies such as Google, Mozilla, Burberry and Code Club.

Our graduates start their careers as Web designers, app developers, UX designers, digital project managers, UI designers, social media producers and game designers among others. They work both within agencies and as freelancers.

The course will provide you with In depth knowledge of the working methods and production processes involved in the creation of web content and services. From passive to active: At Web Media you will develop an in-depth knowledge of the methods involved in the creation of web content and services.

    Meet the Course Leaders

    Developing a pioneering new course at Ravensbourne which teaches social media management skills, viral Web marketing, online community design, how to work with UGC and participant users, ARGs and participatory narratives. Activities involve writing and delivering lectures, tutorials, workshops and practical assessments; preparation of course documents including validation specifications; presentations at academic and business conferences; everyday management of course delivery; plus interviewing and student recruitment.
    I have been creating with computers since I was 3, using my dad’s work machine to make abstract drawings. And that’s what I still do, more or less: making abstract things with code. Between then and now, I trained as a communication designer, taught myself how to program computers and made educational games for clients like the BBC. I teach people aged 7 to 60+ how to be creative with code.
    I'm currently developing work-based learning programmes as well as lecturing on the BA (Hons.) Web Media Production course at Ravenbourne. I specialise in social media and hold a PhD from University of Westminster, where I explored content creation and online sharing. I worked for over 23 years as a graphic and moving image designer in broadcast and the creative industries, where I produced a variety of creative content including title sequences, information graphics, promotions, corporate videos and pop promos.
